Konfiguration

# Process step Expected behavior
1 Define buffer date in odoo settings Next invoice date on sale order subscription in odoo is 30 days + buffer date after create subscription on WH/OUT
2 Go to shop, put abo product in checkout Find Mollie payment method
3 Choose Mollie payment method Redirect to Mollie payment window
4 Pay in Mollie window Customer is created in Mollie payment with selected payment method is created in Mollie mandate is created in Mollie
5 Go to sale order in backend Related invoice which is posted with mollie payment state in Paid
6 Click create Abo WH/OUT WH/OUT is created
7 Go to related Abo WH/OUT Smart Button : Abo Delivery :- Creating Delivery with storable product which linked with abo products.
8 Validate WH/OUT Create Mollie subscription button appears
9 On sale order in odoo click on Close to end subscription invoice creation in odoo and in Mollie as well Subscription in Mollie is created
10 Go to the invoice click on Add credit note confirm that invoice and click on create mollie credit note. Mollie subscription is ended monthly invoices are not created anymore
11 On sale order in odoo click on Recharge Invoice In odoo chatter the log is created with the refund amount. In mollie refund is created for particular payment
12 Go to the related invoice and click on Create customer recharge In odoo chatter the log is created with the recharge amount, sequence type and customer mollie id. Recharge is created in mollie with invoice total amount
